Privacy Policy

Stantique · Last updated: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stantique ("we", "us", "our") handles data in connection with our website and our use of the Pinterest API. We are a small independent vintage and antique resale business. You can reach us at any time at sudeteoantique@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

Stantique operates an online storefront selling our own vintage and antique inventory. We use the Pinterest API solely to publish photographs of our own products to our own Pinterest business account as marketing for our shop.

2. What data we collect

Our website does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels, and does not ask visitors to create accounts or submit personal information.

Through the Pinterest API we access only data belonging to our own connected Pinterest account: our boards and the Pins we create. We do not collect, request, or store personal data of any other Pinterest users. If you email us, we receive the contents of that email and your address, which we use only to reply to you.

3. How long we keep data

Pinterest access tokens for our own account are stored locally only for as long as needed to publish our Pins, and are deleted when no longer required. Emails you send us are kept only as long as needed to handle your enquiry.

4. How we use data

We use the Pinterest API exclusively to create and schedule Pins of our own products on our own account ("API posting"). We do not perform analytics on, profile, or build datasets about other users. We do not use Pinterest data for advertising targeting.

5. Pinterest-derived data and log-out

Any data obtained from Pinterest relates only to our own account. If our account is disconnected or we log out of Pinterest, we stop using the Pinterest API and delete any locally stored Pinterest access tokens and Pinterest-derived data.

6. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell, rent, share, or otherwise transfer Pinterest content or any Pinterest-derived data to third parties. Data is used only by us, for the purpose described above.

7. Your choices and contact

If you have any question or request regarding data — including access, correction, or deletion — contact us at sudeteoantique@gmail.com and we will respond.

8. Use of the Pinterest API and affiliation

This website and application use the Pinterest API. Stantique and this application are not endorsed by, sponsored by, or affiliated with Pinterest, Inc. "Pinterest" is a trademark of Pinterest, Inc.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent change.
